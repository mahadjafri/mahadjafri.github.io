04 — About

A bit about me

I build production systems across analytics, machine learning, and applied AI. Over the past six years, my work has focused on models that survive contact with real users, real ops people, and real workflows.

I recently joined SadaPay as an Analytics Engineer, where I work across analytics infrastructure, financial reporting automation, and self-serve tooling for operational and executive teams.

Before SadaPay, I spent a year at Careem (Uber) building agentic AI systems and LLM-powered tooling for live mobility operations. Prior to that, I spent five years at Afiniti building Bayesian decision systems that drove an estimated $20M in incremental revenue across telecom clients including Telefónica Spain (work that earned top-10% performance recognition four years running).

Right now, I'm most interested in applied AI systems and evaluation-driven LLM workflows, particularly systems that combine strong data foundations with real-world automation.

Longer term, I'd like to point this kind of work toward areas closer to me personally, widening access to healthcare and education for people who've had less access to both.
